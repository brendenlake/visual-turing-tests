Visual Turing Test: Generating new concepts (unconstrained)

We asked people to design new characters, like those that might appear in a foreign alphabet (Chinese, Bengali, Sanskrit, etc.). We also gave this same task to a computer program that simulates how people design new characters.

Can you tell which grid of images was drawn by 4 different people and which grid was drawn by a computer program?

People drew their characters with the following constraints:
  • They were asked to draw characters that do NOT resemble any characters or symbols they already know (from English or any other language).
  • They were only given 3 seconds to draw each character.
  • They drew with their computer mice.

You will see a sequence of N displays just like this one below. In each display, the computer program's grid of images is randomly chosen to appear either on the left or the right. You will always be asked to indicate which grid the computer drew, using your keyboard's arrow keys. Please answer as accurately as you can.
